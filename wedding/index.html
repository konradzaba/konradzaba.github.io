Travel & Accomodation

If you are arriving by plane, the closest airport to the wedding venue is Kraków-Balice Airport im. Jana Pawła II (KRK). From there, we recommend taking a train from the Kraków Airport station to the Kraków Main Station.

If you are arriving by train, the closest train station to the wedding venue is the Kraków Main Station.

If you are arriving by car, we would like to inform you that there are 20 parking spaces available at the wedding reception venue. If you wish to leave your car there after the end of the party, you can collect it only from 12:00 CET on Sunday when the restaurant opens.


After the wedding ceremony we will provide bus transportation to the wedding reception venue. The bus will be available at the intersection of Łobzowska and Garbarska streets.
You can also get to Willa Fryderyka by tram from Teatr Bagatela station to Biprostal station (5 mins ride), or take a taxi.

The choice of hotels and apartments is extensive in Kraków. We recommend to look for accomodation you like within and around picturesque Old Town using Booking.com.

We have also made a block reservation until August 10th for three options for accomodation only for the night of the wedding at special rates (double room, two single beds):

  • Hotel Polonez (higher price, it includes parking and breakfast)
  • Student dormitory Hotel Nawojka (cheap)
  • Student dormitory Hotel Piast (cheap)

We will provide free transportation to these places after midnight.

If you wish to stay at any of these places, please reach out to us for details.
